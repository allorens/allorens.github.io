The MadMusic project aims to investigate the musical practices developed in Madrid over more than six centuries, addressing the entire value chain in the recovery of musical heritage. This includes the study, cataloging, and digitization of musical events and their sources, as well as their modern recovery and preservation through editions, studies, and recordings. It also focuses on their dissemination to society through public performance, while examining the spaces where these practices took place in their architectural and acoustic dimensions, as well as audience reception.

Institutions: Universidad Complutense de Madrid (UCM), Instituto Complutense de Ciencias Musicales, Universidad Politécnica de Madrid, Universidad Nebrija
